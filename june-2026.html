Executive Summary
Strong CX · refunds under watch
June overview
  • June was a strong customer experience month. CSAT reached 4.84 with 95.5% satisfaction, Trustpilot improved to 3.9, and the new FAQ plus return portal created a smoother customer experience.
  • The new FAQ and return portal are already showing positive signs. No negative feedback about the portal/process was observed, exchanges are being completed through the portal, and customer tone in CSAT/Trustpilot improved.
  • The main risk is refunds. Total customer refunds were $27,093.77, equal to 7.06% of June order revenue. The main drivers were cancellation requests and returning products.
Key wins / shortfalls
  • Win: FAQ revamp implemented on the website.
  • Win: New return portal implemented and positively received.
  • Win: CSAT improved to 95.5% satisfaction.
  • Win: Trustpilot improved to 3.9 and should continue toward 4.0.
  • Win: Suzie hired and will also take over Merrhic's position.
  • Shortfall: US 3PL logistics transition is still under negotiation.
  • Shortfall: Some products went out of stock, but it was handled well and did not impact CSAT.

Performance Read

Customer service quality was strong in June. 62 out of 67 scored surveys were 5-star, giving the team a 95.5% satisfaction rate.

The only limitation is sample size: 67 scored surveys is low compared with total monthly ticket volume, so the result is positive but should not be treated as the full customer base.
